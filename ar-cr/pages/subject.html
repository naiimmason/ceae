Instructions:

Please read these instructions carefully and do not communicate with any one while you are making your decisions.

  • Every decision you make involves a certain amount of money that you receive to perform a task.
  • The decisions you make are 'Yes' or 'No' decisions.
  • At the end of the study a random draw will select one of the tasks.
  • We will also ask you to fill out a survey at the end.

Implementation of 'Yes' decisions

  • If you selected 'Yes' to the randomly chosen task, you must perform the task and receive the amount of money you agreed to (performing the task will be done in a shielded area to ensure privacy). This money will be added to the $15 we will pay you for your time.

Implementation of 'No' decisions

  • If you selected 'No' to the randomly chosen task, you will not perform the task but also not receive the amount of involved with the task. You will only receive the $15 we will pay you for your time.
