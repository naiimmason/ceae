Welcome to an experiment in decision-making

Throughout the course of this experiment you will have an opportunity to earn cash based on the choices you make.

You will be asked a series of Yes or No questions regarding your willingness to expose yourself (3 exposures) to various mixtures of water containing Arsenic or Chromium and be paid for doing so.

The three exposures are as follows:

  1. Submerge hand in randomly drawn mixture of water for 10 seconds
  2. Breathe in the vapors of a randomly drawn mixture of water for 5 deep breaths
  3. Drink 2oz of a randomly drawn mixture of water
