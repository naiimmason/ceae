Consent to participate in the study "Novel application of a second-price auction"

PURPOSE/DESCRIPTION OF THE RESEARCH

  • You are invited to participate in a research study about the willingness of people to perform various tasks. This research is being conducted by Kent Messer at the University of Delaware.

PROCEDURES AND CONDITIONS OF SUBJECT PARTICIPATION

  • Over the next 60 minutes, we will ask you to make a series of decisions about whether you are willing to do certain tasks. We will ask you how much we would need to pay you to do these things.
  • Unless we tell you otherwise ahead of time, all the decisions you make will be kept confidential.
  • If you intentionally disrupt the experiment, you may be asked to leave.
